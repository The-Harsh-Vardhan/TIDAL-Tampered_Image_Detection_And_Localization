How the Pipeline Works

Three-stage forensic analysis from raw image to tamper localization

Step 01

Error Level Analysis

Re-saves image at Q=75, 85, 95 producing a 9-channel forensic compression signature.

Step 02

Neural Segmentation

UNet with ResNet-34 encoder processes the 9-channel input through pixel-level classification.

Step 03

Tamper Localization

Generates binary mask highlighting tampered regions with per-pixel confidence scoring.
